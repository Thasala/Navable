Voice settings

Navable prefers backend multilingual transcription for automatic Arabic and English detection, and falls back to the browser speech API if the backend is unavailable.

AI / OpenAI

AI answers and summaries are on-demand. The AI mode controls page-summary behavior when AI is enabled. Voice transcription may also use the backend when multilingual command recognition is available.

Keyboard shortcuts

These are configured in Chrome’s Extensions shortcuts page:

  • Scroll down / Scroll up
  • Next heading / Previous heading
  • Toggle mic

Chrome → Extensions → Keyboard shortcuts.

Privacy

Navable collects a structured snapshot of the current page (titles, headings, links, buttons, and non-sensitive form field labels). It does not collect passwords or credit card fields. Short microphone clips for spoken commands may be sent to your configured backend for transcription, and page snapshots may be sent for summarization when those features are enabled.
